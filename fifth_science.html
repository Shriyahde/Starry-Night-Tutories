Fifth Grade Science

In Fifth Grade, students take a Science EOG (End of Grade Test) which asks them of their science knowledge learned that semester. These are the topics in 5th grade science:

  • Weather
  • Force and Motion
  • Matter and the 3 states
  • Characteristics of Organisms
  • Structures and Functions of organisms

Weather

In Weather, students learn about many different science items. They learn about types of clouds, the Jet Stream, El Nino and La Nina, weather tools, warm, cold, occluded and stationary fronts, what causes the seasons, the Gulf Stream, prevailing westerlies, and weather v.s. climate.
