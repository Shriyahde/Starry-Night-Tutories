Fifth Grade Math

Students attend math classes and are expected to learn specific areas during the school semester. At the end of the year, students take a Math EOG to test their mathematics skills of 5th grade. Be sure to study with your child so you can have higher grades on this.
Units in Math are:

  • Decimals
  • Place Values
  • Rounding numbers
  • Multiplication and Division
  • Volume
  • Coordinate Planes
  • Classifying Figures and Shapes
  • Line plots
  • Adding and Subtracting Fractions, and Multiplying and Dividing Fractions
  • Conversions
  • Numerical Expressions
